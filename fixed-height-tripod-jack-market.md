<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Multi Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fixed height tripod jack market consists of two primary types: single-stage and multi-stage. Single-stage tripod jacks are designed with a fixed height and cannot be adjusted. These jacks are commonly used in simple tasks that require a stable and secure lifting solution. On the other hand, multi-stage tripod jacks offer height adjustability and can be extended to different levels. They are ideal for complex tasks that involve lifting objects to various heights. Both types cater to different needs in the market and offer diverse lifting capabilities.</p></p>
