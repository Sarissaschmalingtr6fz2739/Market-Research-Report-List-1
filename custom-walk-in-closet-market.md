<p><p>In the custom walk-in closet market, there are two main types: Closed Closet and Open Closet. This type of closet is popular for those who prefer a more minimalist and clutter-free style. On the other hand, Open Closet designs have shelving and hanging rods that are visible and accessible without doors, giving a more open and spacious feel. This type is favored by those who want easy access to their clothing and accessories.</p></p>
