<p><p>Vibration Isolation Workstations are designed to reduce vibrations in sensitive equipment or instruments to maintain accuracy and precision. There are two main types of Vibration Isolation Workstations: Active and Passive. Active Vibration Isolation Workstations use sensors and actuators to actively counteract vibrations in real-time, while Passive Vibration Isolation Workstations utilize materials like rubber or air springs to absorb and dampen vibrations without the need for external power. Both types offer effective solutions for various industries requiring stable work environments.</p></p>
