<p><ul><li>Continuous Single Chain</li><li>Continuous Double Chain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bucket elevators are commonly classified into two main types - Continuous Single Chain and Continuous Double Chain. Continuous Single Chain bucket elevators use a single strand of chain to lift or lower materials, offering a cost-effective solution for vertical conveying. On the other hand, Continuous Double Chain bucket elevators use two strands of chain for more heavy-duty applications and increased capacity. Both types are widely used in various industries such as agriculture, mining, and manufacturing for efficiently moving bulk materials vertically.</p></p>
