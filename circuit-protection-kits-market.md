<p><p>Circuit protection kits include various types of components to protect electrical circuits from overcurrent and overvoltage conditions. The types of components included in these kits are circuit breakers, electronic fuses, power thyristors, and others. Circuit breakers are designed to automatically switch off the flow of electricity when a fault is detected. Electronic fuses provide overcurrent protection by breaking the circuit when the current exceeds a certain limit. Power thyristors are used for high-power applications to control voltage and current flow.</p></p>
