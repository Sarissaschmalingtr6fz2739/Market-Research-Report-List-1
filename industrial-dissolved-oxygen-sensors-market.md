<p><p>Industrial dissolved oxygen sensors are used to measure the concentration of dissolved oxygen in various industrial processes. There are two main types of industrial dissolved oxygen sensors: optical and electrochemical. </p><p>Optical dissolved oxygen sensors use fluorescent technology to measure the oxygen concentration, while electrochemical sensors rely on a chemical reaction to generate an electrical signal proportional to the oxygen concentration. </p><p>Each type of sensor has its own advantages and limitations, making them suitable for different industrial applications based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
