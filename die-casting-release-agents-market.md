<p><strong>The Die Casting Release Agents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based Release Agents</li><li>Water-free Release Agents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Die casting release agents are essential for the smooth extraction of casted parts from molds. Water-based release agents are composed mainly of water and additives, making them environmentally friendly and easy to clean. On the other hand, water-free release agents are solvent-based and provide better lubrication and longer tool life. Both types offer different benefits and are used based on specific requirements in the die casting industry. The choice between water-based and water-free release agents depends on factors such as mold material, part complexity, and desired finish.</p></p>
