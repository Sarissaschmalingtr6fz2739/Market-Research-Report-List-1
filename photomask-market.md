<p><p>A photomask, also known as a reticle, is a thin, translucent sheet of glass or quartz that contains the pattern of a circuit to be printed on a silicon wafer during the manufacturing process of semiconductors. Photomasks are essential in photolithography, a key step in semiconductor fabrication that involves transferring the circuit pattern onto the wafer.</p><p>The global photomask market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.9% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for smaller and more powerful electronic devices, leading to a rise in the production of semiconductors. The photomask market is also being driven by the growth of the semiconductor industry in emerging economies, technological advancements in photomask manufacturing, and the increasing investment in research and development activities.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the photomask market include the development of advanced photomask designs to meet the requirements of next-generation electronic devices, the adoption of multi-beam mask writers for high-resolution patterning, and the increasing use of photomasks in various applications such as MEMS (micro-electro-mechanical systems) and LED (light-emitting diode) manufacturing.</p></p>
