<p><p>Automotive Xenon Headlights market is segmented into Low Beam Lights and High Beam Lights. Low Beam Lights are designed to illuminate the road ahead without blinding oncoming drivers, providing a wider and shorter beam pattern. High Beam Lights, on the other hand, are used for increased visibility at long distances on open roads, but should be switched off in the presence of other vehicles to prevent glare. Both types of headlights utilize xenon gas to produce a brighter and whiter light compared to traditional halogen headlights.</p></p>
