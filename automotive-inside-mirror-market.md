<p><p>The automotive inside mirror market is segmented into three main types: plane mirror, concave mirror, and convex mirror. Plane mirrors provide a flat reflection, while concave mirrors curve inward to magnify images and provide a wider field of view. Convex mirrors, on the other hand, curve outward to provide a wider field of view with a smaller image. Each type of mirror serves a specific purpose in enhancing visibility and safety for drivers, depending on the vehicle's design and intended use.</p></p>
