<p><p>Spray dryers are categorized into three main types based on their operation: Pressure Spray Dryers use high pressure to atomize the liquid feed, Stream Spray Dryers operate with a high-velocity stream of air to atomize the liquid, and Centrifuging Spray Dryers utilize a spinning disk to atomize the liquid. Each type of spray dryer has its own advantages and is suitable for different applications, depending on factors such as the nature of the feed material and desired product properties.</p></p>
