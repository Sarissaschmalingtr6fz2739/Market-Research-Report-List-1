<p><strong>The Earphone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dynamic</li><li>Electrostatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dynamic earphones use a traditional driver technology that relies on a magnetic field to generate sound. These are the most common type of earphones on the market and are known for their affordability and versatility. On the other hand, electrostatic earphones use a more advanced technology that involves the use of static electricity to produce sound. These earphones are typically more expensive and are known for their high-quality sound performance, making them popular among audiophiles and professionals.</p></p>
