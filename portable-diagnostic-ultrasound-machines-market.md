<p><p>Portable Diagnostic Ultrasound Machines are available in various modes such as A-mode (Amplitude mode) which measures the intensity of the echo signal, B-mode (Brightness mode) which creates a two-dimensional image, C-mode (Color mode) which adds color to the image for better visualization, F-mode (Flow mode) which examines blood flow, and M-mode (Motion mode) which displays movement over time. Each mode offers different benefits and applications in medical diagnostics, providing healthcare professionals with versatile tools for clinical evaluation and decision-making.</p></p>
