<p><p>Micro-current beauty instruments are categorized into two types: Pull Type and Wheel Type. Pull Type instruments work by producing electrical micro-currents that stimulate the facial muscles through a series of pulling actions. This helps to lift and tone the muscles, promoting a more youthful appearance. On the other hand, Wheel Type instruments utilize tiny wheels that rotate on the skin's surface, delivering micro-currents that reduce fine lines and wrinkles, improve product absorption, and boost circulation for a healthier complexion. Both types of instruments effectively enhance skin rejuvenation and are popular in the beauty industry.</p></p>
