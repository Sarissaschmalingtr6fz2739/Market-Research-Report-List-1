<p><p>Passenger car lubricants market includes three main types: Engine oil, transmission fluids, and brake fluids. Engine oil is used to lubricate and protect the engine components, ensuring smooth operation and prolonging the engine's lifespan. Transmission fluids are essential for seamless gear shifting and reducing friction in the transmission system. Brake fluids help maintain optimal braking performance by transferring force from the brake pedal to the wheels. These lubricants are crucial for maintaining the overall efficiency and performance of passenger cars.</p></p>
