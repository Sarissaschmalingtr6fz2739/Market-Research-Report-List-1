<p><p>Leakage butterfly valves are commonly available in two main types: manual and pneumatic. Manual butterfly valves are operated by a hand lever or gear mechanism, allowing for simple and easy control of flow. Pneumatic butterfly valves, on the other hand, use compressed air or gas to automate the opening and closing process, providing quicker and more precise control. Both types cater to different market demands, with manual valves being more cost-effective and versatile, while pneumatic valves offer enhanced performance and efficiency in industrial applications.</p></p>
