<p><ul><li>Below 6mm</li><li>6mm-18mm</li><li>Above 18mm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flexible plywoods are categorized based on their thickness into three main market types: below 6mm, 6mm-18mm, and above 18mm. Plywoods below 6mm are typically used for decorative purposes and in furniture making where flexibility and lightweight are important. Plywoods in the 6mm-18mm range are commonly used in various construction and interior design applications due to their balance of strength and flexibility. Plywoods above 18mm are commonly used in heavy-duty construction projects where greater thickness and strength are required for structural support.</p></p>
