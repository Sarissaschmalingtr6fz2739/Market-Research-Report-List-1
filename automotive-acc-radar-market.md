<p><p>The Automotive ACC Radar market consists of two main types: OEM and Aftermarket. OEM refers to radars that are installed by the original equipment manufacturer during production of the vehicle. These radars are typically integrated into the vehicle's design and are calibrated to work seamlessly with other systems. On the other hand, Aftermarket radars are installed by third-party vendors after the vehicle has been purchased. These radars offer flexibility in terms of customization and upgrades but may not always be as seamlessly integrated into the vehicle's existing systems.</p></p>
