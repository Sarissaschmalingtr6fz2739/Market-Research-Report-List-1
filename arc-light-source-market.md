<p><ul><li>Continuous-output Xenon Short-arc Lamps</li><li>Continuous-output Xenon Long-arc Lamps</li><li>Xenon Flash Lamps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The arc light source market consists of three main types: continuous-output xenon short-arc lamps, continuous-output xenon long-arc lamps, and xenon flash lamps. Continuous-output xenon short-arc lamps provide a stable and consistent light output ideal for applications requiring constant illumination. Continuous-output xenon long-arc lamps offer a higher power output compared to short-arc lamps, making them suitable for more demanding lighting requirements. Xenon flash lamps, on the other hand, produce intense bursts of light quickly, making them ideal for applications such as photography, stroboscopes, and medical equipment.</p></p>
