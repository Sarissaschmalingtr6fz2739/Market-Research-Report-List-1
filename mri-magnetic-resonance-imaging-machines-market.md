<p><p>MRI machines are used in medical imaging to produce detailed pictures of internal organs and tissues. There are two main types of MRI machines based on their magnet technology: Permanent Magnet MRI and Superconducting Magnet MRI. Permanent Magnet MRI machines use a permanent magnet to create a magnetic field, while Superconducting Magnet MRI machines use a superconducting magnet cooled with liquid helium to generate a stronger magnetic field. Both types have their own advantages and applications in the medical imaging field.</p></p>
