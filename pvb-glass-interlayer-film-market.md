<p><p>Standard Polyvinyl Butyral (PVB) is a widely used interlayer film in the glass industry that provides both safety and security benefits. It is commonly used in applications such as automotive windshields and architectural glass due to its high impact resistance and ability to hold shattered glass together. On the other hand, Structural PVB is a specialized type of PVB that is engineered for structural glazing applications, providing enhanced strength and durability to the glass. Both types of PVB offer different properties and cater to specific market demands.</p></p>
