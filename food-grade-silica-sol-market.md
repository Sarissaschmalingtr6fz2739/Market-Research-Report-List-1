<p><ul><li>Precipitated Silica</li><li>Fumed Silica</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Food Grade Silica Sol market consists of two main types: Precipitated Silica and Fumed Silica. Precipitated Silica is produced through a chemical process, resulting in a fine white powder that is used in a variety of food applications as a flow agent, anti-caking agent, and thickening agent. On the other hand, Fumed Silica is produced through a high-temperature vapor phase hydrolysis process, resulting in a fine, fluffy white powder that is used in food applications for its high surface area and excellent thickening and anti-settling properties.</p></p>
