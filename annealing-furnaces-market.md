<p><p>Annealing furnaces are commonly divided into two main types: batch type and continuous type. Batch type annealing furnaces are designed to process materials in discrete batches, allowing for greater control over the annealing process for individual pieces. Continuous type annealing furnaces, on the other hand, are designed for continuous production, making them more efficient for larger volume operations. The choice between batch and continuous type annealing furnaces depends on the specific production requirements and desired level of control over the annealing process.</p></p>
