<p><p>Research steam sterilizers market consists of three main types: Gravity Cycle, SFPP Cycle, and Pre-Vac Cycle. Gravity Cycle sterilizers rely on gravity to remove air from the chamber, SFPP Cycle sterilizers utilize pressure pulsing to improve air removal, and Pre-Vac Cycle sterilizers use a vacuum cycle to remove air before sterilization. Each type offers distinct advantages and is suitable for different applications. Understanding the differences between these types is crucial for choosing the most appropriate steam sterilizer for specific needs.</p></p>
