<p><p>High performance sputtering targets are used in various industries such as electronics, aerospace, and automotive for thin film deposition. There are two main types of targets- plane targets and rotating targets. Plane targets are stationary and are typically used for small-scale production or research applications. Rotating targets, on the other hand, spin during the sputtering process, allowing for more uniform deposition and higher throughput, making them ideal for large-scale industrial applications requiring high productivity and quality control.</p></p>
