<p><p>Capacitor foil market types include Cathode Foil and Anode Foil. Cathode Foil is used as the negative electrode in electrolytic capacitors, providing a surface for the chemical reactions that store and release energy. Anode Foil, on the other hand, serves as the positive electrode in these capacitors, facilitating the flow of electric current. Both types of foil play crucial roles in the functioning of capacitors by helping to store and discharge electrical energy efficiently.</p></p>
