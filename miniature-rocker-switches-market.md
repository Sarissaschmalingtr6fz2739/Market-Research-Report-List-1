<p><ul><li>SPST</li><li>SPDT</li><li>DPST</li><li>DPDT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Miniature rocker switches come in various types based on their functionality. SPST switches have a single pole, single throw configuration, making them simple on/off switches. SPDT switches have a single pole, double throw design, allowing them to control two separate circuits. DPST switches have a double pole, single throw setup, enabling them to control two independent circuits simultaneously. Lastly, DPDT switches feature a double pole, double throw arrangement, allowing them to control two circuits with multiple potential configurations.</p></p>
