<p><p>Sports bras are categorized into three main types based on the level of support they provide: Light Support, Medium Support, and High Support. Light support bras are suitable for low-impact activities like yoga and walking. Medium support bras offer more compression and are ideal for activities like hiking and cycling. High support bras are designed for high-impact activities like running and intense workouts, providing maximum support and minimizing bounce. Each type caters to different levels of activity and impact, ensuring women can find the right support for their workout needs.</p></p>
