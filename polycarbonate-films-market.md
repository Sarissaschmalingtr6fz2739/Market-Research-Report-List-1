<p><p>Polycarbonate films are categorized into three types: optical, flame-retardant, and weatherable. Optical polycarbonate films have excellent clarity and transparency, making them suitable for applications such as displays and optics. Flame-retardant polycarbonate films offer fire resistance and are commonly used in electronics and automotive industries. Weatherable polycarbonate films are designed to withstand harsh outdoor conditions, providing UV resistance and durability. These films are commonly used in construction, signage, and automotive applications. Each type of polycarbonate film serves specific purposes and caters to different industries with their unique characteristics.</p></p>
