<p><p>Ankle monitors are electronic devices used to track the location and movements of individuals on pre-trial release or probation. There are two main types of ankle monitors on the market: Radio Frequency (RF) and Global Positioning System (GPS) monitors. RF monitors track the wearer's movements within a certain range of a receiver, while GPS monitors use satellite technology to provide real-time location tracking. Both types of ankle monitors serve the same purpose of monitoring and enforcing the wearer's movement restrictions.</p></p>
