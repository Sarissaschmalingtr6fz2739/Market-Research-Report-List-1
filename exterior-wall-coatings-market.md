<p><ul><li>Water-soluble Coatings</li><li>Solvent Coatings</li><li>Emulsion Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Exterior wall coatings can be classified into three main types: Water-soluble coatings, Solvent coatings, and Emulsion coatings. Water-soluble coatings are environmentally friendly and easy to clean, making them ideal for exterior walls. Solvent coatings provide a hard and durable finish but can be toxic and release harmful volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Emulsion coatings offer a good balance between durability and ease of application, making them a popular choice for many exterior wall coating applications. Each type has its own unique characteristics and benefits, catering to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
