<p><strong>The HVDC Transmission System Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Overhead Transmission</li><li>Underground Transmission</li><li>Subsea Transmission</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HVDC transmission systems are utilized in overhead transmission for long-distance power transmission, underground transmission for urban areas or environmentally sensitive regions, and subsea transmission for connecting offshore wind farms or oil platforms to the onshore grid. Overhead transmission is commonly used for high capacity and long distance transmission, while underground transmission is preferred for minimizing visual impact and reducing land usage. Subsea transmission is crucial for transmitting power across bodies of water efficiently and reliably.</p></p>
