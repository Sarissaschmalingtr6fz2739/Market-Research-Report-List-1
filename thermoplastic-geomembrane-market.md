<p><p>Thermoplastic Geomembrane Market Types include HDPE, LLDPE, PVC, and Others. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) geomembranes offer high chemical resistance and durability. LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) geomembranes provide flexibility and puncture resistance. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) geomembranes are known for their UV resistance and weldability. Other types of geomembranes include fPP (flexible Polypropylene) and TPO (Thermoplastic Polyolefin) which offer unique properties such as high tensile strength and cost-effectiveness. These materials cater to various application needs in the geomembrane market.</p></p>
