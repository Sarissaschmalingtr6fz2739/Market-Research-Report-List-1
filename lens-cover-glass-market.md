<p><p>Lens cover glass market is segmented into three main types: synthetic sapphire, tempered glass, and PMMA. Synthetic sapphire offers superior scratch resistance and durability, making it ideal for high-end cameras and smartphones. Tempered glass provides a balance between durability and cost, making it a popular choice for mid-range devices. PMMA, also known as acrylic glass, is more affordable and lightweight, but offers lower scratch resistance compared to synthetic sapphire and tempered glass. Each type caters to different market segments based on their specific needs and requirements.</p></p>
