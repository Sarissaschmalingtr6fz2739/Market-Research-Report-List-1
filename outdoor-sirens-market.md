<p><p>There are three main types of outdoor sirens available in the market: directional, rotating, and omnidirectional. Directional sirens emit sound waves in a specific direction, making them ideal for notifying people in a particular area. Rotating sirens have a rotating horn that creates a pulsed sound pattern, making them effective for reaching a wide area. Omnidirectional sirens emit sound waves in all directions, ensuring that everyone in the vicinity is alerted to an emergency situation. Each type of siren serves a different purpose based on the coverage area needed.</p></p>
