<p><p>Metal caster market is divided into two main types - Swivel caster and Rigid caster. Swivel casters have a rotating wheel attached to a bracket, allowing for 360-degree movement. They are commonly used in shopping carts, office chairs, and furniture. Rigid casters, on the other hand, have a fixed wheel and are best suited for applications that require straight-line movement. They are often found on heavy machinery, carts, and dollies. Both types offer different benefits depending on the specific needs of the application.</p></p>
