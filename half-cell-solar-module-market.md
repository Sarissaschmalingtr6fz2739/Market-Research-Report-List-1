<p><p>A half-cell solar module is a type of photovoltaic panel where the traditional solar cells are cut in half, resulting in smaller current paths and reduced resistive loses. Half-cell solar modules are preferred for large-scale solar installations and commercial projects due to their higher efficiency and reliability.</p><p>The half-cell solar module market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.2% during the forecast period. Overall, the market for half-cell solar modules is poised for significant growth in the coming years as more countries and industries embrace renewable energy solutions.</p></p>
