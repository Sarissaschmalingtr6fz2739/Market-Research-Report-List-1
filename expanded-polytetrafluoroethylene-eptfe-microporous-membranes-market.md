<p><p>Expanded Polytetrafluoroethylene (ePTFE) Microporous Membranes are commonly available in two types based on pore sizes - Micron and Submicron. Micron membranes have larger pore sizes ranging from 1 to 5 microns, making them suitable for applications such as filtration of large particles. On the other hand, Submicron membranes have smaller pore sizes ranging from 0.1 to 0.5 microns, making them ideal for filtration of finer particles and bacteria. Both types of membranes offer high performance in terms of durability, chemical resistance, and permeability.</p></p>
