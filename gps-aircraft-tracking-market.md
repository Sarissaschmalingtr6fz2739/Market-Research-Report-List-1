<p><p>GPS aircraft tracking systems are available in two main market types: portable GPS and fixed GPS. Portable GPS systems are small, handheld devices that can be easily moved between aircraft or used in emergencies. Fixed GPS systems are permanently installed in an aircraft and provide continuous tracking and monitoring capabilities. Both types of systems offer real-time location data, flight information, and communication capabilities to enhance safety and efficiency in the aviation industry. The choice between portable and fixed GPS depends on the specific needs and requirements of the aircraft operator.</p></p>
