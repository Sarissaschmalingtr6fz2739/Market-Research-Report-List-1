<p><p>Renewable Energy Storage Systems are categorized into three main types based on the storage mechanism: Mechanical, Electrochemical, and Thermal. Mechanical storage systems include technologies like pumped hydro, compressed air energy storage, and flywheels. Electrochemical systems utilize batteries and fuel cells to store and convert energy. Thermal storage systems use methods like molten salt or ice to store and release energy. Each type of storage system has its own benefits and applications in storing renewable energy for future use.</p></p>
