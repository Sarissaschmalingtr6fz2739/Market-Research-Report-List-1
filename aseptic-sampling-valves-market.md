<p><p>Aseptic sampling valves are primarily classified into two types - pneumatic and manual. Pneumatic sampling valves are operated using compressed air, allowing for quick and automated sampling processes. This type is ideal for large-scale operations where efficiency is crucial. On the other hand, manual sampling valves require physical manipulation by an operator, providing more control and precision in smaller-scale applications. Both types cater to different needs in the aseptic sampling industry, offering varying levels of automation and user flexibility.</p></p>
