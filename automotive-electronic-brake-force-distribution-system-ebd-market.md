<p><p>The Automotive Electronic Brake Force Distribution System (EBD) market is divided into two main types based on the type of brakes used: Disc Brake and Drum Brake market. Disc Brake systems distribute brake force evenly on all wheels, ensuring effective and smooth braking. Drum Brake systems work by applying pressure on the brake shoes inside the brake drum to slow down and stop the vehicle. Both types of brake systems are crucial components in ensuring safe and efficient braking in automotive vehicles.</p></p>
