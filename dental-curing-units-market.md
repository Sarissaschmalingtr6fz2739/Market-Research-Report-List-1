<p><p>The dental curing units market consists of three types: LED, UV, and Halogen. LED curing units use light-emitting diodes to emit a specific wavelength of light that quickly hardens dental materials. UV curing units utilize ultraviolet light to initiate a polymerization reaction and cure dental restorations. Halogen curing units emit a bright white light produced by a halogen bulb to cure dental materials. These different types of curing units provide dental professionals with options for efficient and effective curing of dental materials.</p></p>
