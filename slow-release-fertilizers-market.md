<p><p>Slow release fertilizers can be classified into different types based on their composition and release mechanisms. Synthetic organic nitrogen fertilizers are composed of organic materials that slowly release nitrogen into the soil, providing nutrients to plants over an extended period. Coated fertilizers have a polymer or resin coating that controls the release of nutrients, ensuring a steady supply to plants. Other types of slow release fertilizers include sulfur-coated urea, controlled release fertilizers, and water-insoluble fertilizers, each offering unique benefits for plant growth and soil health.</p></p>
