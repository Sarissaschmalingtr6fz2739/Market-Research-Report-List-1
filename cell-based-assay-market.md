<p><p>Cell-based assay is a technique used in biological research and drug discovery to examine cellular behavior and response to various stimuli. It involves the use of living cells in an artificial environment to test the effects of drugs, chemicals, or genetic modifications on cellular function. Cell-based assays provide valuable information on toxicity, efficacy, drug-target interactions, and other crucial parameters for drug development.</p><p>The global cell-based assay market has been experiencing significant growth due to factors such as increasing research and development activities in the pharmaceutical and biotechnology industries, rising prevalence of chronic diseases, and advancements in technology. Additionally, the growing demand for personalized medicine and the shift towards drug repurposing strategies have also contributed to market growth.</p><p>The market is witnessing various trends that are influencing its growth trajectory. One such trend is the increasing adoption of 3D cell culture models in assay development. These models provide a more accurate representation of in vivo conditions, enabling researchers to obtain better results. Another trend is the growing application of stem cell-based assays for drug discovery and toxicity testing. Stem cells possess the ability to differentiate into various cell types, making them ideal for studying disease mechanisms and evaluating drug candidates.</p><p>Moreover, the cell-based assay market is experiencing a rise in collaborations and partnerships between pharmaceutical companies and research institutions. This collaboration helps in the development of innovative assays and facilitates the sharing of knowledge and resources.</p><p>According to the provided information, the cell-based assay market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.4% during the forecast period.
