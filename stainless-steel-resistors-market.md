<p><p>Stainless Steel Resistors are categorized into two main types - Linear Resistors and Non-Linear Resistors. Linear Resistors have a linear relationship between voltage and current, resulting in a constant resistance value. Non-Linear Resistors, on the other hand, have a varying resistance value depending on the voltage or current passing through them. Each type is used in different applications based on the specific requirements of the circuit. Both types play a crucial role in various electronic devices and systems.</p></p>
