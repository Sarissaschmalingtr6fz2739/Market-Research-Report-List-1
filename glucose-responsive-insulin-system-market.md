<p><p>The glucose responsive insulin system market consists of different types of insulin based on their action duration. Rapid-acting insulin works quickly to lower blood sugar levels. Short-acting insulin starts working within 30 minutes and lasts for a few hours. Intermediate-acting insulin takes longer to start working but lasts longer. Long-acting insulin provides a steady level of insulin for an extended period. Premixed insulin combines different types of insulin for both short and long-term control of blood sugar levels.</p></p>
