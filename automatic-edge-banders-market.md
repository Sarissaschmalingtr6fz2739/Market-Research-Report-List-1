<p><ul><li>Full-automatic</li><li>Semi-automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Full-automatic edge banders are machines that require minimal human intervention and can perform all the edge banding processes automatically. They are efficient in high-volume production environments. Semi-automatic edge banders, on the other hand, require some manual intervention during the edge banding process. They are suitable for low to medium volume production or for smaller workshops. Both types of edge banders offer different levels of automation and are chosen based on the specific production needs and budget constraints of the user.</p></p>
