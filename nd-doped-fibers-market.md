<p><strong>The Nd Doped Fibers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Clad Fiber</li><li>Double Clad Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nd doped fibers are commonly used in high-power fiber lasers and amplifiers. Single clad fibers consist of a core doped with neodymium surrounded by a single layer of cladding material. Double clad fibers have an additional inner cladding layer surrounding the doped core, allowing for better pump absorption and higher power handling capabilities. Single clad fibers are simpler in design and are typically used in lower power applications, while double clad fibers are more complex but offer higher performance in high-power laser systems.</p></p>
