<p><strong>The Ocular Genetic Diagnostic Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Next-generation Sequencing (NGS)</li><li>Multiplex Ligation-Dependent Probe Amplification (MLPA)</li><li>Fragment Analysis PCR for Repeat Expansion Analysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ocular Genetic Diagnostic market utilizes various techniques for accurate testing and diagnosis. Next-generation Sequencing (NGS) is a high-throughput method that can sequence multiple genes simultaneously, providing comprehensive genetic information. Multiplex Ligation-Dependent Probe Amplification (MLPA) is used to detect copy number variations or deletions in a gene. Fragment Analysis PCR for Repeat Expansion Analysis is employed to identify expansions or repeats in specific genetic sequences, which are common in certain ocular disorders. These techniques play a crucial role in identifying genetic causes of ocular diseases and guiding treatment decisions.</p></p>
