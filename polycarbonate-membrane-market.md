<p><p>Polycarbonate membrane market consists of two main types: hydrophilic and hydrophobic membranes. Hydrophilic polycarbonate membranes have a high affinity for water and are commonly used in applications where water transport is desired. On the other hand, hydrophobic polycarbonate membranes are water repellent and are used in applications where water resistance is necessary. Both types offer unique properties and advantages for different applications, leading to their widespread use in industries such as biotechnology, pharmaceuticals, and water treatment.</p></p>
