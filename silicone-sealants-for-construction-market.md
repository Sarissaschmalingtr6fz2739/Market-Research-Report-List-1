<p><p>Silicone sealants for construction are available in two main types: one-component and two-component. One-component sealants are ready to use and cure when exposed to moisture in the air, making them convenient for quick applications. Two-component sealants require mixing of two separate components before application, offering a longer working time for more precise and complex projects. Both types provide excellent adhesion, weather resistance, and durability, making them popular choices for sealing and bonding in the construction industry.</p></p>
