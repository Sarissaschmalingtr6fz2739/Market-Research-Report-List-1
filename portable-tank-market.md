<p><p>Portable tanks come in two main types: Horizontal Tanks and Vertical Tanks. Horizontal tanks are designed to store liquids or gases horizontally, making them ideal for transportation and storage in tight spaces. They are commonly used for transporting fuel, oil, or water. Vertical tanks, on the other hand, are designed to store liquids or gases vertically, taking up less ground space. They are often used for storing chemicals, fertilizers, or other industrial fluids.
