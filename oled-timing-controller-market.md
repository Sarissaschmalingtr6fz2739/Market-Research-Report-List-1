<p><p>An OLED Timing Controller is a crucial component in OLED displays. The two main market types for OLED Timing Controllers are Independent Type and Integration Type. Independent Type controllers are stand-alone devices that operate separately from other system components, offering flexibility and customization options. Integration Type controllers are built-in to the display panel itself, providing a more compact and streamlined solution. Both market types cater to different needs and preferences within the OLED display industry, with Independent Type offering more versatility and Integration Type offering a more integrated solution.</p></p>
