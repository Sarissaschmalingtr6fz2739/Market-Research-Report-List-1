<p><strong>The E-beam Evaporation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Pocket</li><li>Rotary Pocket</li><li>Linear Pocket</li></ul></p>
<p><p>E-beam evaporation systems are used for thin film deposition in various industries. There are three main types of e-beam evaporation systems based on the number of pockets: Single pocket systems have one crucible for material deposition, Rotary pocket systems have multiple pockets that can be rotated for different materials, and Linear pocket systems have multiple linearly arranged pockets for simultaneous deposition of multiple materials. Each type offers different advantages and is chosen based on the specific needs of the application.</p></p>
