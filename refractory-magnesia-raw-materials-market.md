<p><p>Refractory magnesia raw materials market can be categorized into three main types: Fused Magnesia, Dead Burned Magnesia, and Others. Fused Magnesia is produced by melting natural magnesite in an electric arc furnace, resulting in a high-quality product suitable for various refractory applications. Dead Burned Magnesia is produced by calcining natural magnesite at high temperatures, creating a product with superior chemical and physical properties. The 'Others' category includes magnesia products that do not fall into the aforementioned types, such as caustic calcined magnesia.</p></p>
