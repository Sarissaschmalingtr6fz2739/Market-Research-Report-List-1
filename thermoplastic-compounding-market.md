<p><p>Thermoplastic compounding market is segmented into three main types: Thermoplastic Polymers, Thermosetting Polymers, and Thermoplastic Elastomers. Thermoplastic polymers are materials that soften when heated and harden when cooled, allowing for recyclability. Thermosetting polymers undergo a chemical reaction when heated, becoming rigid and non-reversible. Thermoplastic elastomers combine the properties of rubber and thermoplastics, offering flexibility and resilience. Each type of material caters to specific applications and industries within the thermoplastic compounding market.</p></p>
