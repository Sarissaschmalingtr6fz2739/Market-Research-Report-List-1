<p><p>Radiocontrast agents are substances used in medical imaging to improve the visibility of tissues and organs. There are four main types of radiocontrast agents: Barium-based, Iodinated, Gadolinium-based, and Microbubble agents. Barium-based agents are used in gastrointestinal imaging, iodinated agents are used in CT scans and angiography, gadolinium-based agents are used in MRI scans, and microbubble agents are used in ultrasound imaging. Each type of agent has specific properties and applications in medical imaging.</p></p>
