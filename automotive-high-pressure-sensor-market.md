<p><p>Automotive High Pressure Sensor market includes two main types: Analog Type and Digital Type. Analog Type sensors measure pressure in a continuous manner, providing a voltage or current output proportional to pressure. On the other hand, Digital Type sensors convert pressure readings into digital signals for more precise and accurate measurements. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and efficiency of automotive systems by monitoring pressure levels in various components such as fuel systems, air conditioning systems, and brake systems.</p></p>
