<p><p>Anti-fingerprint coatings, such as hydrophobic and oleophobic coatings, are designed to repel fingerprints, dirt, and oils from surfaces to keep them clean and smudge-free. Hydrophobic coatings repel water and liquids, making it easier to clean surfaces and prevent water damage. Oleophobic coatings repel oils and greases, reducing fingerprints and smudges. Both types of coatings are popular in various industries, including electronics, automotive, and household appliances, where maintaining a clean and sleek appearance is important.</p></p>
