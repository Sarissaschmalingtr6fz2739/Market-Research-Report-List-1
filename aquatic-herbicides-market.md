<p><ul><li>Glyphosate</li><li>Imazapyr</li><li>Diquat</li><li>Triclopyr</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aquatic herbicides market can be categorized into types such as Glyphosate, Imazapyr, Diquat, and Triclopyr. Glyphosate is a broad-spectrum herbicide effective against a wide range of aquatic weeds. Imazapyr is known for its long-lasting residual effects, making it a popular choice for controlling submerged and emergent vegetation. Diquat is a contact herbicide that quickly kills targeted plants upon contact. Triclopyr is effective in controlling woody plants and brush in aquatic environments. Each type of herbicide serves a specific purpose in managing aquatic weed growth.</p></p>
