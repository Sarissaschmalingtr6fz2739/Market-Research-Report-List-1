<p><p>Cholesterol test kits are available in two types: test strip kits and analyzer kits. Test strip kits involve a simple process where a drop of blood is placed on a test strip and then inserted into a portable device that provides the cholesterol level result. Conversely, analyzer kits are more advanced and require a lab or healthcare setting. They consist of a machine equipped with specialized software to measure cholesterol levels accurately. Both kits aim to provide quick and convenient cholesterol testing options for individuals.</p></p>
