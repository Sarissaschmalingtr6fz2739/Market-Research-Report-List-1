<p><p>Control relays are used in various industries for switching and controlling electrical currents. Impulse relays are used for momentary switching operations, such as turning on lights. Monitoring relays are used to monitor different parameters like voltage, current, and temperature to ensure the proper functioning of equipment. Signal conditioning relays process and amplify signals to make them suitable for further analysis or use. Each type of relay serves a specific purpose in controlling electrical circuits and ensuring efficient operation in different applications.</p></p>
