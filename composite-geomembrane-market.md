<p><p>Composite Geomembrane Market includes various types such as HDPE, LLDPE, PVC, FPP, and others. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) geomembranes are durable and resistant to chemicals and UV exposure. LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) geomembranes are flexible and have high puncture resistance. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) geomembranes offer excellent chemical resistance. FPP (Flexible Polypropylene) geomembranes are lightweight and easy to install. Other types of geomembranes may include materials like EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) or XR-5. Each type has its own unique properties and applications in the market.</p></p>
