<p><p>N-channel MOSFET transistors come in two main types based on their operation: enhancement type and depletion type. Enhancement type transistors require a positive voltage at the gate terminal to allow current to flow from the source to the drain, while depletion type transistors conduct current by default and require a negative voltage at the gate terminal to block current flow. Both types have specific applications in different electronic circuits, with enhancement type transistors being more commonly used in digital and analog circuits.</p></p>
