<p><p>Espresso machines are available in three main market types - manually operated, semi-automatic, and fully-automatic. Manually operated machines require the user to control the entire brewing process, including grinding the beans and stopping the extraction. Semi-automatic machines have some automated features, such as a pump to control water flow. Fully-automatic machines handle every aspect of brewing with the push of a button, including grinding, tamping, and extraction. Each type offers varying levels of control and convenience for making espressos.</p></p>
