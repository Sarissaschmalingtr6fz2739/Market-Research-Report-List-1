<p><p>Iodide Ion Selective Electrodes are used for measuring the concentration of iodide ions in a solution. There are three main types of Ion Selective Electrodes: Crystal Membrane, Liquid Membrane, and PVC Membrane. Crystal Membrane electrodes use a solid crystal membrane to selectively detect iodide ions. Liquid Membrane electrodes contain a liquid ion-exchanger that selectively binds to iodide ions. PVC Membrane electrodes have a polyvinyl chloride membrane that specifically reacts with iodide ions. Each type of electrode offers unique advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
