<p><strong>The Head-Up Display System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Windshield Projected HUD</li><li>Combiner Projected HUD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Head-Up Display (HUD) System market can be categorized into two main types: Windshield Projected HUD and Combiner Projected HUD. Windshield Projected HUD systems display information directly onto the windshield in the driver's line of sight, providing essential data without requiring the driver to look away from the road. On the other hand, Combiner Projected HUD systems use a separate display screen or combiner to project information onto the windshield, offering a similar benefit of keeping the driver's focus on the road ahead.</p></p>
