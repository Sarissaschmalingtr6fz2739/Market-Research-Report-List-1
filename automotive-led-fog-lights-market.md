<p><p>Automotive LED fog lights come in two main types: front fog lights and rear fog lights. Front fog lights are installed in the front of a vehicle to improve visibility in foggy or misty conditions. They help drivers see the road more clearly and make it easier for other vehicles to see them. Rear fog lights, on the other hand, are mounted on the rear of a vehicle and are intended to make it more visible to other drivers in low-visibility situations. Both types of fog lights use LED technology for better efficiency and performance.</p></p>
