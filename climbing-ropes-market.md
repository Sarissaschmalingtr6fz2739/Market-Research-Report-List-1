<p><p>Climbing ropes are available in various types of treatments to enhance their performance and durability. Dry core treatment ropes have a water-resistant core, while dry core and sheath treatment ropes have both the core and outer sheath treated for water-resistance. Dry sheath treatment ropes only have the outer sheath treated. Non-dry treatment ropes do not have any water-resistant treatment. Each type of treatment offers different levels of protection against moisture, extending the lifespan of the climbing rope.</p></p>
