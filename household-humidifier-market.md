<p><p>Household Humidifier Market is segmented into three types: Ultrasonic Humidifiers, Direct Evaporative Humidifiers, and Thermal Evaporative Humidifiers. Ultrasonic Humidifiers use ultrasonic vibrations to produce a fine mist, making them quiet and energy-efficient. Direct Evaporative Humidifiers add moisture to the air by passing it through a wet wick filter, suitable for arid climates. Thermal Evaporative Humidifiers use heat to evaporate water, dispersing moisture evenly throughout the room. Each type offers unique benefits catering to different household needs.</p></p>
