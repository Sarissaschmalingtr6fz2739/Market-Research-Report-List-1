<p><p>The automotive composite materials market can be broadly classified into three main types: carbon, glass, and others. Carbon composites, known for their high strength and lightweight properties, are widely used in sports cars and luxury vehicles. Glass composites, on the other hand, are more affordable and commonly found in mainstream vehicles. The "others" category includes composite materials like aramid and natural fibers, which are less common but offer unique characteristics such as impact resistance and sustainability. Each type of composite material caters to different automotive needs and applications.</p></p>
