<p><ul><li>Bottled Water Dispenser or Bottled Water Cooler(BWC)</li><li>Point-of-Use(POU)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Drinking fountains, also known as water dispensers, come in two main market types: Bottled Water Dispensers or Bottled Water Coolers (BWC) and Point-of-Use (POU) systems. BWCs use pre-packaged bottles of water that are placed on top of the cooler, while POU systems are directly connected to a water supply source. BWCs are typically used in homes and small businesses, while POU systems are more commonly found in larger commercial and industrial settings due to their convenience and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
