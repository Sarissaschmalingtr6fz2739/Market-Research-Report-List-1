<p><strong>The Medical Humidity Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Relative Humidity Sensor</li><li>Absolute Humidity Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical humidity sensors are essential in various healthcare settings to monitor moisture levels. Relative humidity sensors measure the amount of water vapor in the air relative to the maximum possible, providing information on comfort and potential risk of mold growth. Absolute humidity sensors, on the other hand, measure the actual amount of water vapor present in the air, offering precise data on respiratory health and infection control. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining optimal humidity levels for patient wellbeing and equipment functionality in medical facilities.</p></p>
