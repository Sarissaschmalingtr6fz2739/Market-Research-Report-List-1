<p><p>Resin-coated Type Actuators are piezo stack actuators where the stack is coated with a resin material for protection against external elements. These actuators are commonly used in applications where the actuator may be exposed to harsh environments. On the other hand, Metal-cased Type Actuators have a metal casing around the stack, providing additional protection and stability. These actuators are ideal for high precision applications where durability and robustness are essential. Both types of actuators serve different purposes based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
