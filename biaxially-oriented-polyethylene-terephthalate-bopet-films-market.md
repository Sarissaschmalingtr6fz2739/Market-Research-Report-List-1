<p><p>Biaxially Oriented Polyethylene Terephthalate (BOPET) Films are categorized based on their thickness: Below 10 um, 10-25 um, 25-75 um, and Above 75 um. Films below 10 um thickness are commonly used for packaging of lightweight products, while those in the 10-25 um range find applications in food packaging and labels. Films between 25-75 um are utilized in industrial and electrical applications. Films above 75 um thickness are used in heavy-duty packaging and construction materials due to their high strength and durability.</p></p>
