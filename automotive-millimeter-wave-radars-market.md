<p><p>Automotive millimeter-wave radars are classified based on their operating frequencies, with the most common types being 24GHz, 77GHz, and 79GHz. The 24GHz radars are commonly used for short-range applications like blind spot detection and parking assistance. The 77GHz radars offer higher resolution and are used for long-range applications like adaptive cruise control. The emerging 79GHz radars provide even higher resolution for advanced driver assistance systems. Other markets in the 80GHz range are also being explored for future automotive radar applications.</p></p>
