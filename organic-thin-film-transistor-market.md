<p><p>Organic Thin Film Transistors (OTFT) have two main types, Organic Field‐Effect Transistors (OFETs) and Organic Electrochemical Transistors (OECTs). OFETs are mainly used for switching and amplifying electronic signals in various applications such as displays and sensors. OECTs, on the other hand, are utilized in bioelectronics and flexible electronics due to their ability to amplify ionic signals. Both types of OTFTs are gaining popularity in the market due to their compatibility with flexible substrates and potential for low-cost production.</p></p>
