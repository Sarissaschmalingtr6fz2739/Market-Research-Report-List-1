<p><p>Veterinary X-ray system market can be categorized into two types: Digital radiography and Analog radiography. Digital radiography uses electronic sensors to capture and store images, offering quicker results and easier image manipulation. This technology is gaining popularity due to its efficiency and improved image quality. Analog radiography, on the other hand, involves traditional film processing and is less commonly used today due to its slower processing time and lower image quality. Both types play a crucial role in diagnosing and treating animals in veterinary medicine.</p></p>
