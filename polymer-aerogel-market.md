<p><p>Polymer aerogels are available in various types including blanket, particle, panel, and monolith markets. Blanket aerogels are flexible and lightweight sheets often used for insulation purposes. Particle aerogels consist of tiny particles that can be used in various applications such as composites and coatings. Panel aerogels are rigid panels commonly used in construction for insulation. Monolith aerogels are bulk materials with high porosity, ideal for applications requiring high surface area. Each type offers unique properties and applications in industries such as construction, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
