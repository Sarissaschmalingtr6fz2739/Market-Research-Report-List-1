<p><ul><li>Parabolic Troughs</li><li>Tower/Heliostat Systems</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Concentrated Solar Thermal technologies primarily consist of three main market types: Parabolic Troughs, Tower/Heliostat Systems, and Other systems. Parabolic Troughs use curved mirrors to concentrate sunlight onto a receiver tube, producing heat for steam generation. Tower/Heliostat Systems use a central receiver at the top of a tower to collect concentrated solar energy. Other systems include dish collectors and linear Fresnel reflectors. These diverse technologies cater to different needs and conditions, offering various advantages and applications in the solar thermal market.</p></p>
