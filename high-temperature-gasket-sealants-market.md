<p><p>High temperature gasket sealants come in two main types: paste and liquid. Paste sealants are thicker and offer more resistance to leakage, making them ideal for applications with larger gaps or irregular surfaces. Liquid sealants are more versatile and easier to apply, making them suitable for smaller gaps and tighter spaces. Both types of sealants are designed to withstand extreme temperatures and pressure, providing a reliable seal for various industrial and automotive applications.</p></p>
