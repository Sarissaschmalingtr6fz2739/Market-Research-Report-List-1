<p><strong>The Polyurethane Reactive Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>Polyurethane Reactive Adhesive market includes two main types: Non-sagging Adhesive and Self-leveling Adhesive. Non-sagging adhesives are designed to hold substrates in place without dripping or sagging during application, making them ideal for vertical or overhead applications. On the other hand, self-leveling adhesives are formulated to spread evenly and create a flat surface, making them suitable for horizontal applications such as flooring installations. Both types offer strong bonding capabilities and are commonly used in construction and manufacturing industries.</p></p>
