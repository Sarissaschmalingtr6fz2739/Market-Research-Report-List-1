<p><ul><li>Float-Feed Carburetor</li><li>Diaphragm Carburetor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Float-feed carburetors are a type of carburetor that uses a float to control the fuel level in the fuel bowl. This type of carburetor is commonly found in older small engines. On the other hand, diaphragm carburetors use a flexible diaphragm to pump fuel into the engine. This type of carburetor is more commonly used in modern small engines. Both types play a crucial role in the functioning of small engines by mixing air and fuel in the correct ratio for combustion.</p></p>
