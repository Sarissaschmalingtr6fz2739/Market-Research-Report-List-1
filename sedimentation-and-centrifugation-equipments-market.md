<p><p>Sedimentation and centrifugation equipments are used for separating solid particles from liquids through different methods. Clarifiers are used to settle out solid particles from a liquid, while disk centrifuges use centrifugal force to separate substances of different densities. Decanters are used for continuous separation of liquid and solids. Dissolved air flotation systems use air bubbles to separate solids from liquids. Hydrocyclones use the centrifugal force to separate particles based on their density. These equipments are essential for various industries for efficient separation processes.</p></p>
