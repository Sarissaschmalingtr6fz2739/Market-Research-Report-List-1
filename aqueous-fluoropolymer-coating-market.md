<p><p>Aqueous Fluoropolymer Coatings are categorized into two types; One Component and Two Component coatings. One Component coatings are ready to use and do not require any mixing before application, offering ease of use. On the other hand, Two Component coatings involve mixing two components before application, providing better performance and adhesion. Two Component coatings are ideal for high-performance applications where durability and resistance are crucial. Both types offer excellent protection against corrosion, chemicals, and weathering.</p></p>
