<p><strong>The Printed Circuit Board Inspection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Automatic Optical Inspection (AOI)</li><li>Automatic X-ray Inspection (AXI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Printed Circuit Board Inspection Equipment market is divided into two main types - Automatic Optical Inspection (AOI) and Automatic X-ray Inspection (AXI). AOI involves using camera systems and software to inspect PCBs for defects such as missing components or misaligned parts. AXI uses X-ray technology to detect faults that are not visible to the human eye, such as hidden solder joints or cracks in components. Both technologies are essential for ensuring the quality and reliability of PCBs in various industries.</p></p>
