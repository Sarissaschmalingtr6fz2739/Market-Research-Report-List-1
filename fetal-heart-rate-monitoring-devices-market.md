<p><p>Fetal heart rate monitoring devices are used during pregnancy to monitor the heart rate of the fetus. There are two main types of devices used for this purpose - Doppler ultrasound devices and electronic fetal monitoring devices. Doppler ultrasound devices use sound waves to detect the fetal heart rate, while electronic fetal monitoring devices measure the heart rate along with uterine contractions. Both types of devices are important tools for healthcare providers to assess the well-being of the fetus during pregnancy and labor.</p></p>
