<p><strong>The Automotive Engine Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Process Sensors</li><li>Position Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Process sensors are used in automotive engines to monitor various parameters such as temperature, pressure, and flow. These sensors provide real-time data to the engine control unit, allowing for precise adjustments in fuel delivery and ignition timing for optimal performance and efficiency. On the other hand, position sensors are used to determine the exact position of engine components like the crankshaft or camshaft. This information is crucial for ensuring proper timing of engine operations such as combustion and valve timing.</p></p>
