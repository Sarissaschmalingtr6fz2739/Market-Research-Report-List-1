<p><p>A triple balanced mixer is a type of mixer that is used in RF (radio frequency) applications to convert two input signals into an output signal with minimal distortion. This type of mixer offers better performance in terms of linearity and noise compared to double balanced mixers. Triple balanced mixers are widely used in various applications such as radar systems, communication systems, and test and measurement equipment.</p><p>The triple balanced mixer market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6% during the forecast period.
