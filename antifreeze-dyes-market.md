<p><p>The antifreeze dyes market is divided into two main types: liquid type and powder type. Liquid type antifreeze dyes come in liquid form and are easily mixed into antifreeze solutions. They are commonly used in automotive applications. Powder type antifreeze dyes, on the other hand, are in powdered form and need to be dissolved in a specific solvent before being added to the antifreeze solution. They are typically used in industrial applications for their ease of handling and storage. Both types offer a convenient way to color antifreeze solutions for easy identification.</p></p>
