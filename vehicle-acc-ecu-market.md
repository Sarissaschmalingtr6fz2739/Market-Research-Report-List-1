<p><p>Vehicle ACC ECU (Advanced Cruise Control Electronic Control Unit) market consists of two main types: OEM and Aftermarket. Aftermarket market includes ECUs that are produced by third-party manufacturers and sold separately for installation in existing vehicles. OEM ECUs are typically more expensive but are designed specifically for the vehicle, while Aftermarket ECUs are more affordable but may not offer the same level of integration or performance.</p></p>
