TTL IC, known for its high-speed operation and low power consumption, is commonly used in industrial applications. CMOS Logic IC is valued for its low power consumption and high noise immunity, making it popular in consumer electronics. BiCMOS Logic IC combines the advantages of both TTL and CMOS, offering high speed and low power consumption, making it suitable for a wide range of applications.</p></p>
