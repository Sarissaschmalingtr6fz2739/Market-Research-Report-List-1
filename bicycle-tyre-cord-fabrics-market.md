<p><p>Bicycle tyre cord fabrics are categorized into three main types: nylon, polyester, and other. Nylon cords are known for their strength, durability, and resistance to abrasion, making them a popular choice for high-performance bicycle tyres. Polyester cords offer similar qualities to nylon but with greater flexibility and resistance to stretch. The "other" category includes a variety of materials such as aramid and rayon, each offering unique properties like lightweight construction or high heat resistance. These different types of cord fabrics cater to the diverse needs and preferences of cyclists.</p></p>
