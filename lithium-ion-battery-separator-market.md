<p><strong>The Lithium-Ion Battery Separator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Layer (PP/PE)</li><li>Bilayer (PP/PE, PP/PP)</li><li>Trilayer (PP/PE/PP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Lithium-Ion Battery Separator Market is segmented into three types: Single-Layer, Bilayer, and Trilayer. Single-Layer separators are made of either polypropylene (PP) or polyethylene (PE). Bilayer separators can be either PP/PE or PP/PP, indicating the combination of materials used. Trilayer separators consist of three layers, specifically PP/PE/PP. These separators play a crucial role in preventing the contact between the positive and negative electrodes of a lithium-ion battery, ensuring its safe and efficient functioning.</p></p>
