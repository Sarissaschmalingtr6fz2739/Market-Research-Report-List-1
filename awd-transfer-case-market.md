<p><p>AWD transfer cases can be categorized into two main types: electronic and manual. Electronic transfer cases are controlled by a computer system which automatically adjusts power distribution to each wheel based on driving conditions. On the other hand, manual transfer cases require the driver to physically switch between 2WD and 4WD modes. Both types have their own advantages and drawbacks, with electronic transfer cases offering convenience and efficiency, while manual transfer cases provide more control and reliability in off-road situations.</p></p>
