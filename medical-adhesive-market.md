<p><strong>The Medical Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li><li>Solids & Hot Melt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical adhesive market is categorized into different types based on their composition and properties. Water-based adhesives are environmentally friendly and easy to clean up. Solvent-based adhesives offer strong bonding capabilities but emit volatile organic compounds. Solids adhesives have high viscosity and are suitable for high-stress applications. Hot melt adhesives are solid at room temperature but melt when heated, providing fast bonding and ease of use. Each type of adhesive has specific advantages and applications in the medical industry.</p></p>
