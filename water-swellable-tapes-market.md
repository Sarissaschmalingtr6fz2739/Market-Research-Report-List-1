<p><p>Water Swellable Tapes are used in the communications industry for protection of cables from water ingress. There are three main types of Water Swellable Tapes based on their conductivity: Conductive, Semi-Conductive, and Non-Conductive. Conductive tapes are used to provide grounding and prevent static electricity buildup. Semi-Conductive tapes provide moderate conductivity for shielding purposes. Non-Conductive tapes do not conduct electricity and are used in applications where conductivity is not required, such as general insulation and cable protection.</p></p>
