<p><p>AC Power Transducers in the market are available in different types based on the number of phases they can measure. Single phase transducers are designed to measure power in single-phase electrical systems. Dual-phase transducers are capable of measuring power in two-phase systems. Multi-phase transducers have the ability to measure power in systems with three or more phases. Each type of transducer caters to specific applications and requirements, providing accurate power measurements for different electrical systems.</p></p>
