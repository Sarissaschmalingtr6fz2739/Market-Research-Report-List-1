<p><p>Silicafumes are a byproduct of producing silicon metal or ferrosilicon alloys. The three main types of Silicafumes market are Densified Silica Fume, Semi Densified Silica Fume, and Undensified Silica Fume. Densified Silica Fume is processed to increase its density, making it easier to handle and transport. Semi Densified Silica Fume is partially processed and falls in between Densified and Undensified Silica Fume. Undensified Silica Fume is in its raw form and requires further processing before use in various applications such as construction, refractories, and oil well cementing.</p></p>
