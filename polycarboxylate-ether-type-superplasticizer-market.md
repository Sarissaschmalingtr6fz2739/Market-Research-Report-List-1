<p><p>Polycarboxylate Ether Type Superplasticizer market is categorized into various types based on their composition, such as TPEG (Triethylene glycol polyethylane ether), MPEG (Monomethoxy polyethylane glycol), HPEG (Hydorxypropyl allyl polyethylane glycol), APEG (Allyl polyethylane glycol), and others. Each type offers unique properties and benefits in improving the workability and performance of concrete.
