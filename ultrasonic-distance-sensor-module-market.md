<p><p>Ultrasonic Distance Sensor Modules are available in two main types in the market: Analog Type and Digital Type. Analog Type sensors provide a continuous output voltage proportional to the distance measured, making them suitable for applications requiring precise distance measurements. On the other hand, Digital Type sensors provide discrete digital output signals that can easily interface with microcontrollers and other digital devices, simplifying the integration process. Both types offer reliable and accurate distance sensing capabilities, catering to a wide range of industrial and consumer applications.</p></p>
