<p><p>The HVAC thermal management market consists of two main types: cooling systems and heating systems. Cooling systems are designed to regulate the temperature of a space by removing heat and lowering the overall temperature, commonly used in warm climates or during the summer months. Heating systems, on the other hand, are used to increase the temperature within a space by generating heat, typically used in colder climates or during the winter months. Both systems play a crucial role in maintaining a comfortable and controlled indoor environment.</p></p>
