<p><p>LTCC components market includes various types such as LC filters, LTCC couplers, dividers/splitters, diplexers, triplexers, chip antennas, and others. LC filters are used to separate signals at specific frequencies, LTCC couplers split power among multiple paths, dividers/splitters divide signals into multiple outputs. Diplexers combine two signals onto one transmission line, triplexers combine three signals. LTCC chip antennas are compact, high-performance antennas for wireless communication. Other LTCC components include isolators, resonators, and baluns.</p></p>
