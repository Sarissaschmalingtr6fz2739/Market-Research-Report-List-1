<p><p>Semiconductor Atomic Layer Deposition Equipment includes two main types: Thermal Atomic Layer Deposition Equipment and Plasma-Enhanced Atomic Layer Deposition (PEALD) Equipment. Thermal ALD equipment relies on heat to deposit thin films onto semiconductor substrates, while PEALD equipment utilizes plasma to enhance the deposition process. Both types of equipment offer precise control over film thickness and uniformity, making them essential tools in the semiconductor industry for producing high-quality, high-performance electronic devices.</p></p>
