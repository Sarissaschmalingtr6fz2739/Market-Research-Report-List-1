<p><p>Epoxy silicone adhesives can be divided into two main types: PSA (Pressure Sensitive Adhesive) and Non-PSA. PSA adhesives are designed to form a strong bond when pressure is applied, making them ideal for applications such as tape or labels. Non-PSA adhesives do not rely on pressure for adhesion, but instead cure through chemical reactions or other means. Both types of adhesives offer different advantages and are used in a variety of industries for bonding different materials.</p></p>
