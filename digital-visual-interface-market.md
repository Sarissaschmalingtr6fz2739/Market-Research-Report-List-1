<p><p>Digital Visual Interface (DVI) has three main market types - DVI-A, DVI-D, and DVI-I. DVI-A supports analog signals, making it compatible with older devices. DVI-D is digital-only, providing high-quality video transmission with no signal loss. DVI-I is a combination of both analog and digital, allowing for greater flexibility and compatibility with various devices. Customers can choose the type of DVI based on their needs, whether they require analog support, digital clarity, or a combination of both.</p></p>
