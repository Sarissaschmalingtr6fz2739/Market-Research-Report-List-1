<p><p>Variable Compression Ratio Actuator market consists of several types such as Multi-link Linkage, Variable Cylinder Head Structure, Variable Piston Top Structure, and Variable Conrod Linkage. Multi-link Linkage systems use multiple linkages to adjust the compression ratio, while Variable Cylinder Head Structure systems alter the cylinder head design to change the ratio. Variable Piston Top Structure systems adjust the piston's shape to vary the compression, and Variable Conrod Linkage systems alter the connecting rods to adjust the ratio. Each type offers unique benefits and considerations for optimizing engine performance.</p></p>
