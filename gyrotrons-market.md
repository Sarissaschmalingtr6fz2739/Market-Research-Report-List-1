<p><p>Gyrotrons are high-power microwave devices used in various applications. Transverse-Output Gyrotrons have the microwave beam output in the transverse direction to the electron beam, making them suitable for applications requiring high efficiency and high power. Axial-Output Gyrotrons, on the other hand, have the microwave beam output in the same direction as the electron beam, making them more suitable for applications requiring precise control and focusing of the microwave energy. Both types of gyrotrons cater to different market needs and applications in the field of microwave technology.</p></p>
