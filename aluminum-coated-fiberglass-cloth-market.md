<p><p>Aluminum Coated Fiberglass Cloth is available in two main types: Double-sided and Single-sided. Double-sided Aluminum Coated Fiberglass Cloth has aluminum coating on both sides, providing enhanced protection against heat, moisture, and chemicals. It is commonly used in applications that require high durability and insulation. On the other hand, Single-sided Aluminum Coated Fiberglass Cloth has aluminum coating on one side only, making it lighter and more flexible. This type is suitable for applications where weight and flexibility are crucial factors.</p></p>
