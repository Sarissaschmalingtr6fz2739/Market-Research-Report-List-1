<p><ul><li>Static Nozzle</li><li>Rotary Nozzle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tank washing nozzles are categorized into two main types: static nozzles and rotary nozzles. Static nozzles are fixed in place and spray water in a single direction, making them suitable for cleaning stationary tanks. On the other hand, rotary nozzles have a spinning mechanism that allows them to cover a larger area and clean tanks more efficiently. They are ideal for cleaning tanks with curved or irregular surfaces. Both types of nozzles play a crucial role in industrial tank cleaning applications.</p></p>
