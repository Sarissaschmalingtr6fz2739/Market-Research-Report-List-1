<p><p>HPMC capsules are available in two main types in the market: HPMC with gelling agent and HPMC without gelling agent. HPMC capsules with gelling agent are designed to provide a slower release of the contents, making them ideal for extended-release formulations. On the other hand, HPMC capsules without gelling agent are mainly used for immediate-release formulations where quick disintegration and release of the contents are required. Both types of HPMC capsules offer unique advantages depending on the specific formulation requirements of the product.</p></p>
