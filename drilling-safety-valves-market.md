<p><p>Drilling safety valves are essential components in the oil and gas industry to prevent blowouts and ensure safe drilling operations. There are different types of drilling safety valves including Kelly Valves, Inside BOP Valve, and Retrievable Drop-In Check Valves. Kelly Valves are located between the drill string and the blowout preventer (BOP) to control pressure during drilling. Inside BOP Valves are used within the BOP stack to provide additional barrier protection. Retrievable Drop-In Check Valves are inserted into the wellbore to prevent fluid backflow.</p></p>
