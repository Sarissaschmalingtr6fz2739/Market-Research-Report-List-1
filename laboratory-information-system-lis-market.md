<p><p>Laboratory Information System (LIS) market consists of two main types: On-premises LIS and Cloud-Based LIS. On-premises LIS refers to software hosted and managed on the organization's own servers, providing control and security but requiring more maintenance. Cloud-Based LIS involves storing data on remote servers accessed through the internet, offering flexibility and scalability without the need for in-house infrastructure. Both types cater to the specific needs of labs and healthcare facilities, with on-premises being more traditional and cloud-based offering modern advantages.</p></p>
