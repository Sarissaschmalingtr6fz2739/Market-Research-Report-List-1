<p><p>Automotive Intake Gas Temperature Sensors are available in several types including Thermistor Type, Thermocouple Type, Semiconductor-Based Type, and Others. Thermistor Type sensors measure temperature by changes in resistance, Thermocouple Type sensors generate voltage based on temperature differences, and Semiconductor-Based Type sensors use a semiconductor material to detect temperature changes. Other types may include optical or infrared sensors. Each type has its own advantages and applications in the automotive industry for monitoring intake gas temperature.</p></p>
