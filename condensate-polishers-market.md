<p><ul><li>Mixed Bed Condensate Polishers</li><li>Cation Condensate Polishers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Condensate polishers are used to remove impurities from power plant condensate water, preventing damage to plant equipment. There are two main types of condensate polishers: Mixed Bed and Cation. </p><p>Mixed Bed Condensate Polishers use a combination of positively and negatively charged resin beads to remove both cations and anions from the water. </p><p>Cation Condensate Polishers use a resin that only removes positively charged ions from the water. Both types of polishers are crucial in maintaining the quality of condensate water in power plants.</p></p>
