<p><p>Long Fiber-reinforced Technical Plastic (LFT) market consists of various types such as LFT-PP, LFT-PA, LFT-ABS, and others. LFT-PP includes long fiber-reinforced polypropylene, which offers high strength and stiffness, making it ideal for automotive and industrial applications. LFT-PA comprises long fiber-reinforced polyamide, known for its excellent thermal and mechanical properties. LFT-ABS consists of long fiber-reinforced acrylonitrile butadiene styrene, providing impact resistance and dimensional stability. Other types in the market may include LFT-PBT, LFT-PET, and LFT-PEEK, among others.</p></p>
