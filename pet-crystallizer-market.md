<p><p>PET crystallizers are essential in the processing of PET flakes into resin pellets. Hot-air Hopper crystallizers are widely used due to their efficiency in drying and crystallizing PET flakes. On the other hand, Infrared Drum (IRD) crystallizers use infrared radiation to heat and crystallize the PET material quickly and uniformly.
