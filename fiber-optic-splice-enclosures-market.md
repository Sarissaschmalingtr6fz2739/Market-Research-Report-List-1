<p><p>Fiber optic splice enclosures are essential components in telecommunications networks for protecting and organizing fiber optic splices. There are two main types of fiber optic splice enclosures: Dome type and Horizontal type. Dome type enclosures are round and designed to be mounted on poles or walls, while horizontal type enclosures are rectangular and are typically installed on the ground or in underground vaults. Both types provide secure and organized protection for fiber optic splices, ensuring reliable communication transmission.</p></p>
