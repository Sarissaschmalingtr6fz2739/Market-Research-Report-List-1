<p><p>Large area LCD displays are classified into three main types based on the technology used for the display: Static, Simple Matrix, and Active Matrix. Static displays are basic and only capable of showing fixed images. Simple Matrix displays have improved resolution and can display simple motion graphics. Active Matrix displays, the most advanced type, have individual transistors for each pixel which allows for high resolution and fast refresh rates, making them ideal for high-quality video and graphic applications.</p></p>
