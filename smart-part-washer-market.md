<p><strong>The Smart Part Washer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-based</li><li>Water/Aqueous-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solvent-based and water/aqueous-based smart part washers are two primary types of cleaning systems used in industrial settings. Solvent-based washers use chemicals like mineral spirits or petroleum-based solvents to remove dirt, grease, and other contaminants from parts. Water/aqueous-based washers, on the other hand, use water as the primary cleaning agent along with detergents or surfactants to break down and remove contaminants. The choice between solvent-based and water/aqueous-based washers often depends on factors like the type of contaminants being removed, environmental concerns, and cost considerations.</p></p>
