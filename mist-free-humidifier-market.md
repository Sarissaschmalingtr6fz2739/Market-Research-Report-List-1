<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Humidifier</li><li>Evaporative Humidifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mist-free humidifiers come in two main types, ultrasonic humidifiers and evaporative humidifiers. Ultrasonic humidifiers use high-frequency vibrations to create a fine mist of water droplets, while evaporative humidifiers use a fan to blow air through a wet wick or filter, evaporating water into the air. Both types are popular for their ability to add moisture to the air without producing visible mist, making them ideal for use in bedrooms, offices, and other indoor spaces where excessive humidity or mist is undesirable.</p></p>
