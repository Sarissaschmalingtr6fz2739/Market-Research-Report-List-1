<p><p>Wireless smart lighting control systems utilize different types of communication protocols to connect and control lighting devices. Zigbee, Wifi, Bluetooth, Z-Wave, and EnOcean are some of the most commonly used protocols in the market. Zigbee is known for its low power consumption and mesh network capabilities. Wifi offers high data transfer speeds for larger networks. Bluetooth provides easy pairing and connectivity for smaller systems. Z-Wave offers secure and reliable communication for smart home applications. EnOcean is known for its energy harvesting capabilities, eliminating the need for batteries.</p></p>
