<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Mutli Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>GPS Simulator market can be classified into two main types: Single Channel and Multi Channel. Single Channel GPS simulators are designed to simulate the signals of a single satellite at a time, making them suitable for basic testing and calibration purposes. On the other hand, Multi Channel GPS simulators can simulate signals from multiple satellites simultaneously, offering more advanced testing capabilities such as scenario-based simulations and interference testing. Both types cater to different user requirements based on the complexity and flexibility of testing scenarios needed.</p></p>
