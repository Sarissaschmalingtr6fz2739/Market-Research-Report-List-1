<p><p>CR Tech Digital X-Ray Imaging refers to Computed Radiography technology where traditional X-ray films are replaced by digital sensors for capturing images. DR Tech Digital X-Ray Imaging, on the other hand, stands for Digital Radiography technology where images are captured directly onto a computer without the need for a cassette or film. Both technologies offer advantages such as faster image processing, improved image quality, and lower radiation doses for patients. These advancements have made digital X-ray imaging popular in medical practices and hospitals worldwide.</p></p>
