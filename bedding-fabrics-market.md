<p><p>Bedding fabrics can be classified into two main categories based on their construction: woven fabric and knitted fabric. Woven fabric is made by interlacing two sets of yarns at right angles to each other, creating a strong and stable fabric. Knitted fabric, on the other hand, is made by interlocking loops of yarn, resulting in a stretchy and flexible material. Both types of fabrics have their own unique characteristics and advantages, catering to different consumer preferences and needs in the bedding industry.</p></p>
