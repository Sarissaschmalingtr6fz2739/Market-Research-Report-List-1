<p><p>Automotive intake systems play a crucial role in regulating the airflow into the engine. Variable Valve Timing (VVT), Continuous Variable Valve Timing (CVVT), Variable Valve Timing with intelligence (VVT-i), and Intelligent Variable Valve Timing and Lift Electronic Control (i-VTEC) are different types of technologies used in intake systems to optimize engine performance and fuel efficiency. VVT adjusts the timing of the intake valves, CVVT continuously varies valve timing, VVT-i adds intelligence to the system, and i-VTEC combines VVT with variable valve lift for enhanced performance.</p></p>
