<p><p>Thermoplastic Polyurethanes (TPU) are versatile materials used in various industries due to their excellent properties such as flexibility, durability, and chemical resistance. The TPU market is categorized into three main types based on the chemical composition: Polyester-based TPU, Polyether-based TPU, and Polycaprolactone-based TPU. Polyester-based TPUs offer high strength and abrasion resistance, Polyether-based TPUs provide superior flexibility and low-temperature performance, while Polycaprolactone-based TPUs offer improved biodegradability and chemical resistance. Each type caters to different applications and markets based on their specific properties.</p></p>
