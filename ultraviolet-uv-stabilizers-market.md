<p><strong>The Ultraviolet  (UV) Stabilizers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hindered Amine Light Stabilizer (HALS)</li><li>UV Absorbers</li><li>Quencher</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UV stabilizers are additives that are used in various industries to protect materials from degradation caused by UV radiation. Hindered Amine Light Stabilizers (HALS) work by trapping free radicals formed during UV exposure, preventing degradation. UV Absorbers absorb UV radiation and dissipate it as heat, protecting the material. Quenchers work by deactivating excited states of molecules formed by UV radiation. Other types of UV stabilizers include antioxidants and metal deactivators, which also help in preventing material degradation due to UV exposure.</p></p>
