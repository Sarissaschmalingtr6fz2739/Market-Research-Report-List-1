<p><ul><li>Half Screw Bolt</li><li>Full Screw Bolt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bolt (fastener) market can be categorized into two main types: half screw bolt and full screw bolt. Half screw bolts have a partially threaded shaft, allowing for secure fastening while also providing flexibility for adjusting the tightening level. On the other hand, full screw bolts have a fully threaded shaft, ensuring a more uniform distribution of pressure when fastening components together. Both types of bolts have their own advantages and are commonly used in various industries for different applications.</p></p>
