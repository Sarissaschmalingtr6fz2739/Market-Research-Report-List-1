<p><p>Food Grade Butylated Hydroxytoluene (BHT) is commonly available in two forms in the market: powder and flaked. Powdered BHT is a fine, granular form that is easily soluble in oil-based products, making it ideal for use in foods such as baked goods and dry mixes. Flaked BHT, on the other hand, is solid and can be easily measured and added to liquid-based products. This form is commonly used in products like oils, fats, and beverages. Both powdered and flaked BHT are widely used in the food industry as antioxidants to prevent oxidation and increase shelf life.</p></p>
