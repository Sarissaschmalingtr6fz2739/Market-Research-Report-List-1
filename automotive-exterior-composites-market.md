<p><p>The automotive exterior composites market includes various types of composites used in the manufacturing of vehicle exteriors. Compression-molded composites are made by placing a combination of fibers and resin into a mold and applying pressure until it hardens. Injection-molded composites involve injecting melted resin into a mold containing reinforcement fibers. Resin-infused composites are created by impregnating reinforcement fibers with resin and then curing them. Furthermore, there are other types of composites used in the automotive exterior market that do not fit into these categories.</p></p>
