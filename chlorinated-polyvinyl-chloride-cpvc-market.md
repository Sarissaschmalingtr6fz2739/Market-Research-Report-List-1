Injection Grade CPVC is commonly used in the manufacturing process of complex shapes and parts through injection molding. On the other hand, Extrusion Grade CPVC is preferred for applications where the material needs to be formed into pipes, tubes, and other continuous shapes through extrusion processes. Both types cater to different market needs and applications within the CPVC industry.</p></p>
