<p><p>Bunker oil market is divided into two main types: residual fuel and distillate fuel. Residual fuel is a heavier oil which is left over after the distillation process, often used for large ships and power plants due to its high viscosity and sulfur content. Distillate fuel, on the other hand, is a lighter, cleaner oil that is commonly used in smaller vessels and vehicles. These two types offer different properties and are chosen based on the specific needs of the consumer.</p></p>
