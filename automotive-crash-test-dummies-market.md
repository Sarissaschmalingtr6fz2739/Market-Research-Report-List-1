Frontal Impact Dummies are used to simulate head-on collisions, Side Impact Dummies are designed to mimic crashes from the side of the vehicle, and Rear Impact Dummies are used to test collisions from behind. Each type of crash test dummy is crucial in assessing the safety and durability of vehicles in different types of crashes.</p></p>
