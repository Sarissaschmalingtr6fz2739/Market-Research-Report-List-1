<p><p>Medical X-Ray Films come in three different types - Green, Half Speed Blue, and Full Speed Blue. Green X-Ray films are designed for general radiographic procedures, providing good image quality and contrast. Half Speed Blue films are designed for procedures with less exposure to radiation, offering better image detail with longer processing times. Full Speed Blue films are used for high-speed procedures, providing excellent image quality in a shorter processing time. Each type caters to specific needs in the medical imaging field, ensuring accurate and reliable results.</p></p>
