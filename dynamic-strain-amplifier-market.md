<p><ul><li>DC</li><li>AC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dynamic Strain Amplifiers can be divided into two types based on their input signal - DC and AC. DC amplifiers are specialized for low-frequency applications where the input signal is a steady direct current. On the other hand, AC amplifiers are designed for higher frequency applications, amplifying alternating current signals. DC amplifiers are commonly used in static strain measurement systems, while AC amplifiers are preferred for dynamic strain measurement systems where rapid changes in strain need to be accurately amplified and monitored.</p></p>
