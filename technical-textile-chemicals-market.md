Colorants are chemicals used to add color to technical textiles, resulting in different shades and patterns. The others category includes a variety of chemicals used for specific purposes, such as flame retardants, antimicrobials, and finishing agents. These chemicals play a crucial role in the production and functionality of technical textiles.</p></p>
