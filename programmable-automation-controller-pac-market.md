<p><p>A Programmable Automation Controller (PAC) can be categorized into three market types: Integrated Type, Distributed Type, and I/O Type. Integrated PACs combine both PLC and PC functionalities in a single device, offering high processing power and flexibility. Distributed PACs are designed for systems with multiple control points, allowing for decentralized control and real-time communication. I/O PACs focus on input/output functions, offering modular design and scalability for various applications. Each market type caters to different automation needs and requirements.</p></p>
