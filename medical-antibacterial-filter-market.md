<p><p>The medical antibacterial filter market consists of two main types: electrostatic and mechanical filters. Electrostatic filters use an electric charge to trap particles and bacteria, providing efficient filtration and purification. On the other hand, mechanical filters rely on physical barriers like fibers to capture and remove contaminants from the air or liquid. Both types are essential in medical settings to prevent the spread of infections and provide clean and safe environments for patients and healthcare professionals.</p></p>
