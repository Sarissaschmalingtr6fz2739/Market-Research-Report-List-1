<p><strong>The Automotive Engine Pulley Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crank Pulley</li><li>Accessory Pulley</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive engine pulleys are classified into two main types: crank pulleys and accessory pulleys. Crank pulleys are located at the front of the engine and are connected to the crankshaft, which converts the reciprocating motion of the pistons into rotational motion. Accessory pulleys, on the other hand, are connected to various engine components such as the alternator, power steering pump, and air conditioning compressor. These pulleys are driven by the crank pulley and help power the auxiliary systems of the vehicle.</p></p>
