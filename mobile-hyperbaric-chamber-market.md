<p><p>Mobile hyperbaric chambers can be categorized into two main types: monoplace and multiplace chambers. Monoplace chambers are designed to accommodate only one patient at a time, making them more suitable for individual treatments in smaller spaces. On the other hand, multiplace chambers are larger and can accommodate multiple patients at once, making them more efficient for group treatments or clinical settings. Both types of chambers offer hyperbaric oxygen therapy, but their design and capacity differ based on the number of patients they can accommodate.</p></p>
