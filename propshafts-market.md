<p><p>Propshafts market is divided into two types: Single Piece Propshaft and Multi-Piece Propshaft. Single Piece Propshafts are typically used in smaller vehicles and comprise of a single shaft connecting the transmission to the differential. On the other hand, Multi-Piece Propshafts are more commonly found in larger vehicles such as trucks or SUVs, consisting of multiple sections connected by joints or couplings for increased flexibility and durability. Both types serve the same purpose of transmitting power from the engine to the wheels efficiently.</p></p>
